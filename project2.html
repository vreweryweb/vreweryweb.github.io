AMuSING

rIDES

A concept pitch for a VR application in amusement theme parks. The brief is to come up with unique experiences that leverage the motion, forces of real world physics while allowing you to witness alternative new worlds. The concepts also include hardware rig setup ideas that will add to the overall user experience.

Scroll Down


By combining VR headset with hardware rigs, a high level of user immersion is achieved. This is evident from the use of VR headsets in existing roller coaster rides. The aim of this design sprint was to come out with quick ideas and illustrate how VR can change the amusement park industry. Experiences like being in space, roller coasting through the solar system, being in the shoes of your favourite super hero, navigating a sci-fi/fantasy maze are all possible in VR world.
